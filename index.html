How I work:


I love tacos more than your average cat. If you find your tummy grumbling and realize that you’re fiending for some tacos anytime of the day or night, all you need to do is pet me by pushing the button below! I’ll crawl all over your city to find the taco restaurants nearest to you!


I’m not the neatest with presenting all of my hard work right now. It’s really hard to write when you don’t have opposable thumbs! Feel free to give me and my development team suggestions on how I can improve!
